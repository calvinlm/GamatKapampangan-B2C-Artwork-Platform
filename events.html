Experience Art, Culture, and Connection

At Gamat Kapampangan, every event is a celebration of Kapampangan artistry and heritage. Immerse yourself in a vibrant showcase of tradition, creativity, and community.

EVENTS

The Heritage Walk

The Heritage Walk is a collaborative guided tour organized by Pamintuan Mansion, Museo ning Angeles, and the Gamat Kapampangan Consumers Cooperative. This event is designed to showcase the richcultural heritage of Angeles City, offering participants an immersive experience into its history and artistry.

Unang Yakap Art Auction

The Gamat Kapampangan Artist Consumers Cooperative, in collaboration with Converge ICT Solutions Inc., hosted the"Unang Yakap Art Auction" at the Garden Wing Ballroom of Edsa Shangri-La. This meaningful event brought together art enthusiasts and advocates to support mothers and children in need, particularly in the Baseco community of Tondo, through Caritas Manila. With the theme "Unang Yakap" or "First Embrace," the event underscored the importance of nurturing and support during a child’s early developmental years. The exhibit featured a stunning collection of artworks created by Gamat Kapampanganartists, each piece reflecting the cultural richness of the Kapampangan heritage.

LUB. LUAL

Richness and vibrancy of Kapampangan culture throughtheir upcoming exhibit, "LUB. LUAL." This highly anticipated artshowcase will be held at the Bale Gamat Kapampangan Art Gallery. The exhibit features the exceptional works of the Gamat Kapampangan Art Group, highlighting their uniqueperspectives and artistic talents. Visitors will have the chance to immerse themselves in captivating artworks that reflect the depth and beauty of Kapampanganartistry.

Pamiabe

At the heart of the exhibit is the iconic“walis tingting,” a traditional broom crafted from resilient twigs. This symbolof cooperation and togetherness represents the power of collective effort—justas communities unite to overcome challenges, so too does the broom cometogether to form something stronger. The “walis tingting” serves as a recurringmotif throughout the exhibit, highlighting themes of teamwork, collaboration,and support.

Tara Na! Muli Tana King Bale Matua

This art exhibit features the incredibleworks of esteemed Kapampangan artists in collaboration with Matuang Bale Bakeryand the Capampangan Art Studio. The exhibition showcases the creative endeavors of local artists who are passionately committed to preserving andpromoting the rich artistic and cultural traditions of the Kapampangancommunity. Each artwork reflects the vibrant heritage and identity thatcontinues to shape our collective story.

Kalilukan Culiat:
Pisamban Maragul

Clark Museum proudly hosts KalilukanCuliat: Pisamban Maragul, a multimedia art exhibition celebrating Angeles City'srich cultural and spiritual heritage. This spectacular event pays homage to thecity's iconic Holy Rosary Parish Church and highlights the vibrant artistry ofthe Kapampangan community.

PANIBAYU

Step into a world of creativity andinspiration at PANIBAYU, a captivating drawing exhibit showcasing the incredible talents of 23 local artists. This exhibition invites you to explorestunning artworks where every line tells a unique story.

Art in the Yard

Immerse yourself in a stunning collectionthat beautifully captures the essence of daily life at Art in the Yard! This exhibition features the thought-provoking works of the talented artistic duo, Lauren Jascha Tolentino and Chelssie Aven Pan.

Kulturang Amana, Sukat Pamana

"Kulturang Amana, Sukat Pamana" at Starmills Robinson in San Fernando, Pampanga, celebrating the rich heritage andtraditions of the Sinukwan Festival. This exhibit is a wonderful opportunity toappreciate the vibrant and diverse cultural heritage of Pampanga, as seen through the eyes of its artists. Don’t miss this chance to experience theessence of Kapampangan culture through art.

A visual artexhibit showcasing the works of various artists, curated by Joel Mallari,highlighting Kapampangan culinary heritage. The exhibit will feature paintings,sculptures, and other artworks related to Kapampangan cuisine.

SITSIT' GULÍSAK

Pamikalugud

Espress-YOU

Enjoy the rich aroma of coffee while discovering local artworks that captivate the senses.

ART STYLE &
INSPIRATION

Danilo Viray is a versatile artist who works in various mediums, including classic, modern, watercolor, oil, pen and ink, and charcoal. His artistic journey began as a commercial artist, displaying and selling his works in prominent galleries along Mabini, Manila. While financial concerns were important, his passion for self-expression also played a key role in his art. He believes that artists express their emotions through their work, even when their feelings aren't fully clear. Danilo emphasizes the importance of humility, perseverance, practice, and patience in being an artist, stating that true artistry is defined by these qualities rather than money or popularity.

ART TO
SOCIETY

From my perspective, painting is not just a passion; it feels like a connection with my soul. As artists, we are much like our creations—every piece of art holds meaning. In my time, art was a luxury that wasn’t easily accessible, and I had to work hard for it. But now, my soul finds joy in painting. What I want to convey is that my life has been fulfilled through art. While I became known, it’s not just about fame. There will always be opportunities for others to rise and be recognized. Even if I’m not there, I know the future holds chances for others to succeed in the art world.
